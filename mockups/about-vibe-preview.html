WHAT WE DO

We make going out
simple.

Going out should be the easy part of a night. The decision, the ticket, the door — three small moments that should disappear into the experience. The tools to make that happen haven’t caught up with how people actually live it.

So we built VIBE — one platform for both sides of the night. Fewer steps for guests. Fewer clicks for organizers. Real support for both. The place where curated nights live.

Our mission is to make going out simple at every step, and to build the marketplace where the best experiences in the region live. We’re starting with RAWDEO 2 in June. One event, one city, one chance to get it right.

Costa Rica first. Then wherever nights worth having are waiting for a better way to happen.
