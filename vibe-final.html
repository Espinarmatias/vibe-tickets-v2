1. Platform Usage

VIBE. CR is operated by VIBE Technologies CR S.A., registered in Costa Rica. By accessing the Platform you agree to these Terms. Use is permitted only for lawful purposes.

2. Ticket Purchase Policy

All sales are final. No refunds except in the event of organizer-initiated cancellation. In that case, full refunds are processed within 10 business days. Tickets are non-transferable unless explicitly permitted. Resale above face value is prohibited and will void the ticket.

3. Payment Processing

All payments are processed securely through ONVO Pay (PCI DSS Level 1). VIBE. does not store payment card information. SINPE Móvil is processed through BCCR infrastructure.

4. QR Code Validity

Each ticket has a unique QR code valid for single entry. Screenshots are not accepted. VIBE. is not responsible for QR codes shared by the ticket holder.

5. Prohibited Activities

Users may not attempt to circumvent security, create fraudulent accounts, scrape data, or resell tickets above face value. Violations result in immediate account suspension.
